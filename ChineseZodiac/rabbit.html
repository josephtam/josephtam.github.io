The Rabbit

Rabbits are seen as peaceful and loving animals, and they represent hope to the Chinese people. The Han people's custom is that a pregnant woman can't eat rabbit because the baby could be born with a harelip. Another custom is that the new baby is given paintings of rabbit to represent a peaceful and happy life.

Strengths: Compassionate and modest, people born in the year of the Rabbit have a good memory and sense of humour. They also work fast and efficiently.

Weaknesses: Bad with money, may appear soft and passive, and can lose chances in life due to a reserved personality.
