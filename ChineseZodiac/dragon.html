The Dragon

In the Chinese culture, the Dragon has a really high reputation. Emperors were known as dragons, and the things associated with their rule such as their thrones and clothes were known as dragon thrones/dragon gowns. They represent luck, honour, authority, and dignity.

Strengths: Natural leaders and perfectionists, they are intelligent, energetic and ambitious. When faced with difficulties, they are never discouraged.

Weaknesses: Can be arrogant, impatient, and over-confident. They are very critical, and do not control their moods, also having a fiery temper.
