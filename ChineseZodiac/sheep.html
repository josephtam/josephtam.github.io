The Sheep

Sheep have always been associated with good things, as they are gentle and calm, and have provided wool to keep us warm for thousands of years. The colour white on them symbolizes good things.

Strengths: Year of the Sheep are sensitive, polite and kind, and prefer quiet lifestyles. They also like to be economical and handle business cautiously.

Weaknesses: Big worriers, and very shy, can be pessimistic and weak willed. Sheep lean on flattery from friends, and don't express love openly.
