The Dog

The dog is known everyone as man's best friend. They get this title because of their ability to understand the humans, and obey their commands. The Chinese culture look at the dog as a lucky animal, and if a dog comes to a house the family would be willing to adopt it as it symbolizes good fortune.

Strengths: Straightforward, faithful, and courageous. They are very trustworthy, and make good leaders. They can also be stubborn, but with the right things.

Weaknesses: Can lack stability, and find fault with many things. They are bothered by unnecessary anxiety.
