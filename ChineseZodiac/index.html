The Chinese Zodiac

About the Chinese Zodiac: Also known as Sheng Xiao, the Chinese Zodiac signs are based on a cycle of twelve years, with each year having its own animal sign. Your sign is determined by the lunar year you were born in. The animal of your birth year is believed to have a profound influence on your behaviours, personality, and your destiny. Chinese astrology deals with the five elements earth, fire, water, metal, and wood, along with Chi, Yin and Yang, and the cycle of time.

Legend: There are a few different legends about the zodiac animals, based on the region in which they are found. In Buddhism, the legend is that Buddha called all the animals on earth to see him off on his departure from this earth, but of all the animals in the world only 12 of them came. As a reward, these 12 animals each got a year named after them, in the order in which they arrived.
