The Pig

Pigs have a bad reputation, everyone knows them to be lazy, dirty, and fat. However, in Chinese culture, pigs were once regarded as a symbol of wealth, and don't have as bad of a reputation as they do in the West.

Strengths: People in the year of the Pig are honest and blunt. They have a sense of calm around them, and have strength. Very loyal and kind friends, and although they are quick tempered they balance it out with their dislike of arguments.

Weaknesses: Can be too trusting and naive and are tricked easily. Don't have many friends, and are not good at communicating with others.
