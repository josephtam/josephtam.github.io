The Rooster

The Rooster; also known as nature's alarm clock. For people all around the world the sound of the rooster was how they started their day. In the Chinese culture, people used to and still worship ancestors and believe in spirits, and the rooster was related to exorcising evil spirits.

Strengths: Bright and capable, Roosters have the strength of deep thinking. They also have strong self respect and don't usually rely on others. Have the tendency to dress up and are known to be pretty or handsome.

Weaknesses: Hot temper, narrow minded, and vain. Their ego gets in the way of personal relationships and have difficulty relating to others. Like to give suggestions but refuse to recieve them.
