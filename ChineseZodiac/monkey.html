The Monkey

The Monkey as we all knows is famous for being clever. As our closest living relative, it is usually compared to a smart person. Long ago a leader of China had the same name as the monkey (Hou), and from then on the monkey was put up on doors and walls to bring good luck to officials.

Strengths: People born in the year of the Monkey are good problem solvers, energetic, smart, and versatile. Have a strong desire to learn and have a good memory. Very sociable, and will put their own work aside to help others.

Weaknesses: Sometimes stand in the way of their own success. Monkeys can be impatient, jealous, and arrogant. Have a tendency to look down on others.
